Biography

Shuya Feng is a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering at UConn. Her academic professor is Yuan Hong. Her research interest is differential privacy and machine learning security. Before studying at UConn, she completed her master's degree from Illinois Institute of Technology, where she graduated in 2017.

Research Interests

  • Differential Privacy
  • Privacy-preserving Machine Learning
  • Distributed Machine Learning Security
  • Data Privacy and Security
